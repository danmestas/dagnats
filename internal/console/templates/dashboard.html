Welcome to the dagnats console

This console is a live window onto the NATS state the engine is already keeping — not a separate database. Everything updates as workflows run, triggers fire, and dead-letters accumulate. No refresh needed.

Operations (Workflows, Runs, Triggers, DLQ) are the day-to-day surfaces. Browse what's registered, watch what's running, retry what failed.

Ops is the deeper view: workers and leases, the KV bucket inspector, an audit log of every operator action, and a metrics dashboard with latency histograms and anomaly markers. Reach for it when operations don't answer.
